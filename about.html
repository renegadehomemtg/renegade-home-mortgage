The Renegade Story

After two decades in the mortgage industry, I built Renegade Home Mortgage to serve West Linn the way I always believed it should be done — with strategy, education, and your long-term goals at the center of every decision.

Meet Michael Neef

I've been working in the mortgage industry since 2005, and one thing has stayed consistent the entire time: most homebuyers are underserved by a system built to benefit lenders, not borrowers. That's what led me to start Renegade Home Mortgage — a different kind of mortgage company built on strategy, education, and transparency.

Over the past two decades, I've helped first-time homebuyers, experienced homeowners, and real estate investors make smarter financing decisions. My approach is simple: I take the time to understand your long-term goals, then structure a financing solution that supports those goals — rather than pushing a single loan product.

I'm also the founder of the Renegade Home Mortgage App, a mortgage technology platform that helps lenders and real estate professionals deliver a modern, transparent experience for borrowers. Through the Renegade Home Mortgage App, I've helped loan officers across the country improve client communication, streamline applications, and elevate the borrower experience.

I believe the mortgage process should be clear, strategic, and centered around education. My goal is to help you understand your options, avoid costly mistakes, and confidently use real estate as a tool for long-term financial growth.

When I'm not helping clients or building software, you'll find me mountain biking the trails around West Linn, working on new tech projects, or spending time with my family right here in the community I serve.

Our Philosophy

Three Promises to Every Client

Total Transparency

Every fee disclosed upfront. Every option explained clearly. No hidden costs, no bait-and-switch, no surprises at the closing table. You'll always know exactly what you're paying and why.

Local Expertise

We know that Bolton's historic homes need different appraisal approaches than Skyline Ridge's newer construction. Our neighborhood-level knowledge translates directly into better loan outcomes.

Personal Service

You'll have one point of contact from application to closing. No call centers, no transfers, no repeating your story to a new person every time. We're a phone call away — even on weekends.

Community

More Than Mortgages

We're deeply invested in the West Linn community — because it's our community too.

WhatsUpWestLinn.com

We created WhatsUpWestLinn.com as a community hub for everything happening in our city — from the annual Old Time Fair at Willamette Park to local school events, restaurant openings, and neighborhood news. It's our way of giving back to the community that gives us so much.

Local Involvement

You'll find us at West Linn High School football games, volunteering at the Chamber of Commerce events, and supporting local businesses from Bolton to Robinwood. When you work with Renegade, you're supporting a business that invests right back into West Linn.
