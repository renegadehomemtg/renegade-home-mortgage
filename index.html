West Linn Real Estate Corner

Market Snapshot

West Linn's 27,000 residents enjoy a median household income of $150K, well above state and national averages. The city's 11 distinct neighborhoods range from historic Bolton and Willamette to the luxury developments of Savanna Oaks and Skyline Ridge. Understanding which loan program fits which neighborhood is key to a smart purchase.
